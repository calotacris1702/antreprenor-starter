Antreprenor Starter

From ancient survival to modern solidarity – Nobody left behind.

In ancient tribes, when wild animals attacked, those left behind – often children – were the most vulnerable. Today, Antreprenor Starter carries that same primal instinct into the digital age: to protect and lift up those just starting out.

About Cristian

After 17 years in the corporate world (and a few spectacular ups & downs), I realised the best way to heal a community is to empower its beginners. Antreprenor Starter is my invitation to co‑create a fair, open playground where efforts are rewarded transparently with the BART symbolic currency.

How can you shape this?

Whether you are 12 or 72, designer or beekeeper, your skill‑set is welcome. Share what you can offer, tell us what you need, and describe the reward you’d like in return – from BART tokens to heartfelt feedback.
